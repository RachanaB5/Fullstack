1. Inside Out 2

Release date: 14 June 2024 (USA)
Director: Kelsey Mann
Box office: $1.693 billion
Distributed by: Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures
Budget: $200 million
Cinematography: Adam Habib, Jonathan Pytko

Review:

Inside Out 2 dives back into Riley's mind, exploring her emotional growth as new feelings emerge. Packed with humor, warmth, and insightful lessons about maturity, this sequel deepens Pixar's beloved exploration of emotional intelligence.

Director: Shawn Levy
Box office: $1.337 billion
Distributed by: Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures
Based on: Marvel Comics

Review:

Rating: ★★★★★ (4.8/5)
